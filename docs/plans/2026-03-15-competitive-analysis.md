## Competitive Landscape Summary

### Where the Market Gap Exists

| Capability | Who Does It | Gap? |
|------------|------------|------|
| AI-powered directional bias (bullish/bearish) | Nobody well | **YES - PRIMARY GAP** |
| Fundamental news aggregation for forex | ForexFactory, FXStreet, Investing.com | Partial - none use AI to synthesize |
| Multi-timeframe bias (1W, 1M, 3M) | Nobody | **YES - UNIQUE** |
| Technical pattern recognition | TrendSpider, Autochartist, TradingView | No gap |
| Social/copy trading | eToro, ZuluTrade, Myfxbook | No gap |
| Economic calendar | ForexFactory (dominant) | No gap |
| Real-time scanning/alerts | Trade Ideas, TrendSpider | No gap |
| AI chat assistant for trading | Kavout, TrendSpider, Investing.com | Emerging |

### Key Insight

No platform currently uses AI (specifically LLMs) to:
1. Scrape and aggregate fundamental news by instrument
2. Generate a synthesized bullish/bearish bias
3. Provide multi-timeframe directional outlook (1-week, 1-month, 3-month)
4. Cover the specific forex/CFD instrument set (DXY, major pairs, crosses, metals, indices)

The closest competitors are ForexFactory (news + calendar, no AI analysis) and FXStreet (analyst opinions, no AI synthesis). TrendSpider's Sidekick LLM is the nearest AI competitor but focuses on technical analysis, not fundamental news synthesis.
